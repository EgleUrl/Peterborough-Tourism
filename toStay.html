Mistletoe House Bed & Breakfast

This hotel is set 2.2 miles from Burghley House Gardens & Deer Park. The hotel is 10 minutes by car from Stamford. Accommodation units at Mistletoe House Bed & Breakfast feature TV set, smoke detectors and a separate toilet. An electric kettle, kitchenware and glassware are also provided in the units.

Mistletoe House Wittering Road, Barnack, Peterborough PE9 3EY

Arman Lodge House

This family-run guest house is in the north of Peterborough, with easy access to junction 18 of the A47. It has free WiFi, free parking and good-value rooms. Arman Lodge is 2 miles from Peterborough Rail Station and the city centre. The shops and museums and the Nene Valley steam railway are within walking distance. There is a taxi company next to the guest house.

3 Scotney Street Newengland, Peterborough PE1 3NG
